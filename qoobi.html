Design

We created the Qoobi as a piece of art and technology. Made from the highest quality materials that can be a best part of any contemporary interior. A sharp shape of anodized hand polished aluminum polyhedron enclosed in a thin quarz glass prism not only looks conceptual and attractive, but performs the the cooling function for the heated vacuum tubes. The visual impression enhances by the interactive LED Light controlled with mobile App.

App

With the stylish Qoobi Mobile App you can control your Qoobi via Bluetooth from any smartphone or tablet. And adds many cool features to your Qoobi such as: Qoobi ambient light adjustment (color, brightness); vacuum tubes temperature and resource level control for a better sound; exclusive Qoobi relax ambient sounds and audiosavers; latest firmware update version. And much more. Available soon in App Store and Google Play.

Qoobi

Qoobi B.V. is a Holland based audio tech start up founded by a community of designers, audiophiles and engineers united by their passion for music.

Our dream is to give people opportunity to wirelessly enjoy music not only on small portable Bluetooth speakers, but to enjoy High-End tube sound on their home stereo systems and feel it like musicians do in the studio!
